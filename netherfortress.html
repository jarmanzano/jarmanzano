Nether Fortress
Home Village Mineshaft Nether Fortress Stronghold
Nether fortresses are large structures consisting of bridges, towers and corridors which can only be found in the nether. It is primarily made of red nether brick. It is the only place where blazes and wither skeletons can naturally spawn and the only place where you can get nether wart, a primary item needed for brewing
